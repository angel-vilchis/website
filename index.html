Hi! I'm Angel Vilchis

About me

All my life is filled with phases where I attempt to learn and be "good" at something: soccer, video games, basketball, musical instruments, video games again, and badminton. I've always appreciated how natural and easy experts make their passion look. But I'm not an expert in any of these activities; I never was. Sooner or later I realized I had to narrow my focus if I wanted to earn the feeling of being an "expert."

Two years ago, as I approached the end of my second year in college, I made a decision to commit myself to Machine Learning for the long term and relinquish the pursuit of being "good" at too many other things. Nowadays, delving into this field feels like a hobby to me.

There is still much more for me to learn and countless ways in which I can grow as a person. But this constant journey of learning and personal growth is what makes it all exciting and enjoyable.
