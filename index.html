About me

My life is filled with phases where I attempt to learn and be "good" at something: soccer, video games, basketball, musical instruments, video games again, and badminton. I've always admired how natural and easy experts make their passion look. But I'm not an expert in any of these activities; I never was. Sooner or later I realized I had to narrow my focus if I wanted to earn the feeling of being an "expert."

Two years ago, as I approached the end of my second year in college, I made the decision to go "all in" on Machine Learning for the long term and sacrifice other areas. Nowadays, this field feels more like a hobby then work.

There is much more for me to learn and countless ways I can grow as a person. But this constant journey of learning and personal growth is what it's all about.
